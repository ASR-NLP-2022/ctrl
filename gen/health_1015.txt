 It isn't as simple as just changing out the oil in the engine. Your body is constantly trying to maintain homeostasis and balance between things like food intake and exercise regimen. If you're using more than normal for some reason, it's going to get harder for you. 
 
 The brain has its own way of keeping track of what you eat and how much water you drink each day. It's not so easy when you take into account that there are many other factors involved with this kind of information. 
 
 This change might seem small, but you'll feel good knowing you've been making an effort towards eating healthier foods.
